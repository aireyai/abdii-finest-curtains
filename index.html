Three things we don't charge for.

No call-out fee. No measuring fee. No installation surprise on the bill. The only thing you pay for is the curtains themselves.

01

Free site visit

We come to your home, office or hotel anywhere in Nairobi. We see the windows in their natural light and advise on style, length and fabric that'll work.

02

Free measurement

Precision measurement by hand, top to floor, across the whole window. We account for pelmet height, pleat style and the way the fabric will break at the sill.

Free installation

Rails, tracks, brackets, the works. We install with the right fixings for your wall, clean up after ourselves and leave the curtains dressed and ready.

Frequently asked

Before you book.

Is the site visit really free?
Yes — no call-out fee, no measuring fee, nothing hidden. We come to your home or office, take measurements, show fabric samples, and you're only committed once you choose to order the curtains themselves.
Which areas of Nairobi do you cover?
We cover all of Nairobi and surrounding suburbs — Eastleigh, Kilimani, Westlands, Lavington, Kileleshwa, Karen, Runda, Parklands, South B and South C included. If you're a little further out, message us and we'll arrange it.
How long from measurement to installation?
Typically 5 to 10 working days depending on the fabric and the size of the job. Blackout and velvet made-to-measure usually takes around a week; larger full-home projects 10–14 days.
What fabrics and styles do you offer?
Sheer linens, blackout, velvet, cotton blends and weighted drapery — in pinch pleat, eyelet, tab-top and wave styles. We bring the swatches to your site visit so you can see colour in your own lighting.
Do you supply rails and tracks?
Yes. Brass rods, silent tracks, curved bay-window tracks and wave systems — included in the quote and installed for free.
Can I visit the showroom?
Please do. We're at Moyale Mall, Shop S26, 4th Street Eastleigh. Monday–Saturday 9am–7pm. Sundays by appointment.
